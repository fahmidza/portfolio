# Renewable Energy Potential Dashboard — Indonesia

> Interactive analytical dashboard visualizing renewable energy potential across Indonesian provinces for strategic energy planning.

## Overview

This project creates a **comprehensive analytical dashboard** that explores the renewable energy potential across Indonesia's diverse provinces. By visualizing solar, wind, hydro, and other renewable sources, the dashboard provides insights for sustainable energy policy development.

## Problem Statement

Indonesia aims to increase its renewable energy share significantly in the coming decades. However, **uneven resource distribution** across the archipelago requires data-driven analysis to identify the most promising regions and energy types. This dashboard addresses that need with visual analytics.

## Tech Stack & Tools

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Google Data Studio / Power BI | Interactive dashboard |
| Python | Data preprocessing |
| Pandas | Data cleaning and aggregation |
| Geographic visualization | Provincial mapping |

## Methodology & Approach

### 1. Data Collection & Preprocessing
- Gathered renewable energy potential data across Indonesian provinces
- Cleaned and standardized metrics for cross-province comparison
- Calculated potential capacity by energy source

### 2. Dashboard Design
- **Provincial comparison** — Side-by-side energy potential rankings
- **Energy type breakdown** — Solar, wind, hydro, geothermal distributions
- **Geographic heatmaps** — Spatial visualization of energy potential
- **Trend analysis** — Historical and projected capacity growth

### 3. Strategic Analysis
- Identified provinces with highest untapped potential
- Compared investment cost-effectiveness by region
- Highlighted infrastructure readiness gaps

## Key Results & Insights

- Mapped renewable energy potential across all major Indonesian provinces
- Identified top regions for each energy source type
- Provided data-driven recommendations for energy investment prioritization
- Visualization enables quick comparison for policy stakeholders
